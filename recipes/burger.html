Burger

Description

A hamburger, often known as a burger, consists of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Patty
  • Any other toppings

Steps

  1. Drop the meat in the pan, squash and hold for 5 seconds.
  2. Grill the buns in the fat from the burger you just squashed until golden brown.
  3. Assemble your smashed burger
